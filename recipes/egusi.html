Egusi

Description

This recipe is to make the delicious egusi soup

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 11/2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
  • 1/2 cup peanut oil
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 habanero peppers
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 pound chopped spinach

Steps

  1. blend pumpking seeds
  2. heat oil and add seasoned beef
  3. blend tomatoes, onions and peppers and add to meat, reduce heat to medium and cook for 40 mins
  4. add water and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. add spinach and ground pumpkin seeds and simmer for 10 more minutes
